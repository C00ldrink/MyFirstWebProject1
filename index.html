10/29/25: Personal Favourite: Sticky Toffee Pudding

Out of all the desserts I have had, Sticky toffee pudding is the best among them.Sticky toffee pudding is an English dessert consisting of a moist sponge cake covered in a toffee sauce, often served with a vanilla custard or vanilla ice cream. Its thick,rich caramel sauce, combined with the exquisite taste of dates makes it among the best desserts in the world! Want to learn how to make it ?

10/25/23: Toffee Pudding Recipe


½ cup (113 g) unsalted butter - cold and cubed
1 cup (200 g) soft light brown sugar
1¼ cups (290 g) heavy cream

1¾ cups (285 g) pitted dates
1 tsp baking soda
1¼ cups (150 g) all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
¼ tsp salt
½ cup (100 g) soft light brown sugar

If you only have one muffin tray you can bake the puddings one batch at a time. Just make sure to get the second batch in as soon as possible. You can bake this sticky toffee pudding as a cake or in larger ramekins as well, you'll just need to adjust the cook time.
